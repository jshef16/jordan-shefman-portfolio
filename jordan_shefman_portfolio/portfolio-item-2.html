Super Mario Pathfinding Game

< Get Mario through the castle to find Princess Peach! >

Given a map of assorted characters that mean different different things, I (Mario) must navigate the "castle" to find Princess Peach. Using different pathfinding algorithms, I found the shortest path to find Princess Peach in any given castle.

The pathfinding algorithms that I used included:

  • Depth First Search
  • Breadth First Search
  • Dijkstra's Algorithm

Each castle layout included multiple rooms, walls that seperated the rooms, and pipes that can transport you from room to room, directly. Some of the pipes do not lead anywhere, and had to be ignored. The walls had openings in them acted as doors. The algorithms moved Mario from room to room, but he could not pass through walls. Through both black-box, white-box, and mutation testing, I proved that the algorithms that I implemented moved Mario from start to finish in the most efficient manner.
